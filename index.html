Introduction to Start Smart

     Start Smart is an educational youth organization that assists youths in Myanmar to choose their career with their passion.
      We are processing with 3 sectors such as education sectors, knowledge sharing sector and volunteering sector.
      Before we started this organization, we planned to run our organization based on 4 programs such as Business Program, Information technology, language programs and scholarship preparation program.
      But when we started running the organization, our start smart team planned to add extra activities which include youth based competitions, youth clubs, donation projects and workshops that assist youths to choose their career to improve the youths’ creative skill and to reach more community development.

Start Smart Organization

  • Objectives
  • Mission
  • Vision
  • From personal development to community development
  • To provide youths the opportunities to learn introduction of their passionate subjects
  • For the youths, to know exactly what subject and career are their passion
  • To the youths in Myanmar get equal opportunity in education
  • To provide work experience to active and passionate youths especially teenagers in a positive work environment and learn effective soft skills for their life career.
  • For the youths, to know exactly what subject and career that they are passionate about, we open classes that are popular among the youth community.
  • To have a useful education in real life for all the youths, we organize workshops and seminars to let the youths learn new social knowledge.
  • For personal development to community development, we are holding fundraising events that can assist youths to choose their career and personal development to donate the needed places in Myanmar.
  • To contribute useful education to all the youths who live both in rural and urban areas within 3 years.
  • To open these online classes as face-to-face short-term classes for the youths who find it difficult to get Internet connection in Myanmar.
  • For our volunteers, to be more confident, smart, have strong teamwork skills and be passionate to contribute positive things and knowledge to our community.

Start Smart Process

Education Sector

We provide 4 programs such as Business program, IT program, language program and scholarship preparing with low fees to learn about the introduction of their passionate subjects and to do further study. We have to create other upcoming programs.

Knowledge Sharing Sector

This sector includes two parts which is sharing contents about career knowledge, useful educational information on our “‘Start Smart” social media platforms and holding workshops that can assist youths in choosing their career and youth development.

Volunteering Sector

Providing the opportunities for the volunteers in Start Smart to get volunteering experience by making organizational skills trainings and working in positive work frame.
