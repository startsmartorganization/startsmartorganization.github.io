Introduction to Start Smart

Start Smart is an educational youth organization that assists youths to choose their career with their passion.

Learn About Us

Start Smart Organization

  • Objectives
  • Mission
  • Vision
  • From personal development to community development
  • To provide youths the opportunities to learn introduction of their passionate subjects
  • For the youths, to know exactly what subject and career are their passion
  • To the youths in Myanmar get equal opportunity in education
  • To provide work experience to active and passionate youths especially teenagers in a positive work environment and learn effective soft skills for their life career.
  • For the youths, to know exactly what subject and career that they are passionate about, we open classes that are popular among the youth community.
  • To have a useful education in real life for all the youths, we organize workshops and seminars to let the youths learn new social knowledge.
  • For personal development to community development, we are holding fundraising events that can assist youths to choose their career and personal development to donate the needed places in Myanmar.
  • To contribute useful education to all the youths who live both in rural and urban areas within 3 years.
  • To open these online classes as face-to-face short-term classes for the youths who find it difficult to get Internet connection in Myanmar.
  • For our volunteers, to be more confident, smart, have strong teamwork skills and be passionate to contribute positive things and knowledge to our community.

Start Smart Process


Education Sector

We provide 4 programs such as Business program, IT program, language program and scholarship preparing with low fees to learn about the introduction of their passionate subjects and to do further study. We have to create other upcoming programs.

Knowledge Sharing Sector

This sector includes two parts which is sharing contents about career knowledge, useful educational information on our “‘Start Smart” social media platforms and holding workshops that can assist youths in choosing their career and youth development.

Volunteering Sector

Providing the opportunities for the volunteers in Start Smart to get volunteering experience by making organizational skills trainings and working in positive work frame.

Success from Struggle Part (1)
3-Day Virtual Fundraising Workshop

27-29th Aug 2021

3:00 - 5:30PM
      (GMT+6:30)

Zoom Meeting

A big career-related 3-Day fundraising virtual workshop where professionals from various fields share their stories, struggles, and how they overcame their hardships before success.
Through this programme, we aim to encourage the youth — regardless of the judgements bombarded by society — to carry on their dream journey, stay consistent to achieve their goals, and keep doing what they love.
For this Part 1 workshop, Start Smart has chosen the “Photography & Film making” field, which will be graced by successful professionals in their respective fields. Additionally, this programme includes Q&A sessions to help participants understand better, plus fun game sessions to keep the participants delighted and joyful.

U Know It?
3-Day Virtual Fundraising Workshop

9-12th Sept 2021

3:00 - 5:30PM
      (GMT+6:30)

Zoom Meeting

“U know it?” workshop is a 4-day virtual workshop. This workshop will be containing 3 categories: Sex Education, Health Care, and Gender Understanding. Sex education sector purposes to increase knowledge of the misconception of the sex education in Myanmar which could help to reduce unwanted events in the community. The Healthcare Sector aims to improve the knowledge of hygiene that is especially related to sexually transmitted diseases (STDs). Gender-based violence (GBV) is a panel discussion sector that will be held by 3 gender various speakers discussing the situation of GBV in Myanmar. The last sector is Sexual Orientation, Gender Identity, and Expression (SOGIE). The goal of it is to offer more information for a better understanding for a variety of genders.

Introduction to SDGs 4-Day Virtual Workshop

"Introduction to SDGs" workshop is a 4-day virtual workshop. The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all" which were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030. We will do the knowledge sharing of SDGs and lots of fun activities in the workshop.There will be two special guest speakers, Mr. Token Aung and Mr. Aung Myat Thu for the workshop. This workshop will be mainly targeted to the youths.
